## OVERVIEW

This interactive dashboard analyzes the current geopolitical conflict between the US-Israel alliance and Iran, and its impact on global financial markets. The analysis focuses on:

- Energy price shock transmission through the Strait of Hormuz blockade
- Sector-specific return projections
- Regional economic impact assessment
- Strategic investment timeline with entry and exit points
- Valuation metrics and risk assessment

## INVESTMENT THESIS

The "Short, Sharp, Shock" hypothesis posits that despite current market panic, the conflict will resolve within 90-120 days, creating a V-shaped recovery opportunity. Key positions include:

- Hold physical gold (target: $5,400/oz by Q4 2026)
- Buy defense sector dip (5-year replenishment cycle)
- Avoid European airlines (unhedged fuel exposure)

- Interactive scenario selector (Short War / Prolonged / De-escalation)
- Dynamic oil price trajectory with confidence bands
- Sector performance heatmaps
